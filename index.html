Welcome to RUN JOJO RUN

This is Jojo! She adores tasty snacks, but she absolutely HATES hoovers!! Henry the Hoover is on the hunt for Jojo, help her gather all the treats before he catches up with her!

  • Each treat will give you 20 points
  • Each tennis ball will give you 50 points. The tennis balls will also place the hoovers back in their starting position.
  • Once you get the tennis ball, the hoovers will start to flash and they won't be able to attack you

Click on START GAME to begin!

How to play:

Jojo is hungry for some treats, help her get all the treats before the hoover gets to her!

  • Each treat will give you 20 points
  • Each tennis ball will give you 50 points
  • Once you get the tennis ball, the hoovers will start to flash and they won't be able to attack you
